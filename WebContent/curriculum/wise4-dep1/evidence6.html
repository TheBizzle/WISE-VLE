What are the elements of the process of a crowd forming a bottleneck?

The elements of the bottleneck process are the students who are crowded at the classroom exit.

How do the elements of the process behave and interact with each other? Give 2 examples.

1) The students each push and shove one another as they try to make progress toward the exit.
2) The students are all simultaneously trying to exit.

By what mechanism do the interactions of the elements cause the pattern?

The mechanism of this processes is the simultaneous action of all the students, each of whom wants the same thing: to get through the door once the bell has rung. The fact that no students began moving to the door until the bell rang assured that a bottle neck would emerge as a result of the simultaneous but independent actions.
