What do engineers do?

Do you know what engineers do? Some engineers crash cars to smithereens. The video below shows a crash-test of a 2002 Acura TL hitting a barrier at 40 mph. (Video provided courtesy of Progressive Auto Insurance Company)

Without the engineers who do these crash tests, you could be seriously injured if you were the passenger in this accident. But thanks to engineers and their research on how to make cars safer, thousands of people's lives are saved each year from car accidents like this one.
