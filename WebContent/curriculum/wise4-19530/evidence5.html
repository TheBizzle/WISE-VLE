Using Computer Simulations

As we saw before, engineers do crash tests to analyze the results from car crashes. Engineers also use computer simulations to see what might happen in a crash.


Here is an example of a simulation created by engineers at Applied Research Associates (http://www.ara.com). These engineers took apart a real car bolt by bolt and constructed it on the computer to help them see what the car would do in a head-on collision.
