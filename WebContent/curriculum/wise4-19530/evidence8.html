Black Box Technology

Have you ever heard of the "black box" that records events in an airplane in case of a crash? Information from the device can be used to reconstruct the events that took place leading up to the crash.

Well, how about using one for a car? Here's an actual product designed to do something similar to the airplane box, made by a company called Road Safety (www.roadsafety.com). How might a device like this help us make cars safer?
