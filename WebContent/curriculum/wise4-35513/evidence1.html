the Discussion

 

In this activity, you will have an online discussion, to provide your suggestions to the Office of Transportation of your county.

The Office of Transportation has been informed that a new type of fuel, hydrogen, is available. Officials are proposing to use hydrogen as fuel in all cars and buses running in the county. Do you agree or disagree with this idea?

You can post your ideas in the online discussion forum in the next WISE step. Remember to post your ideas AND your reasoning. Your discussions should consider at least the three factors:

  • Environment
  • Cost
  • Safety

After you finish with your posting, you should read and reply to at least two other students' postings, and give reasons why you either agree or disagree with their opinions.
