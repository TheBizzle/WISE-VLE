Energy Sources

Most energy we use comes from chemical energy stored in three sources: NATURAL GAS, COAL and OIL.

Natural gas, coal and oil are called FOSSIL FUELS. How are fossil fuels formed?

  • They were formed deep under the earth during dinosaur times.
  • Plants and small animals took the energy from the sun and stored as chemical energy.
  • Plants and animals died and were buried underground. Over millions of years, they were compressed to make fossil fuels.
