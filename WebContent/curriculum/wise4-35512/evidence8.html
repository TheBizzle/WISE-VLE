Inside a fuel cell

Fuel cell technology uses hydrogen to produce electrical power. One fuel cell could generate enough electrical power to light a bulb. When a lot of fuel cells are combined, they could generate electrical power enough to power a car.

Hydrogen and oxygen react and the released heat changes to electrical energy stored in fuel cells to power cars.
