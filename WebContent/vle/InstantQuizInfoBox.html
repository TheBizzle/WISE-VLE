Instant Quiz
  • An Instant Quiz contains one or more questions that allow you to self-monitor your understanding. You'll receive immediate feedback as you answer each question.
  • After successfully completing each question you can click Next Q to move on to the next question. Or click Previous Q to review your solution to an earlier question.
  • Grading can be ON or OFF. If grading is on, you'll receive points as you complete each question. If grading is OFF there are no points, just instant feedback indicating if you are correct or incorrect.
  • An Instant Quiz can contain a variety of question types including Multi-Choice, Multi-Answer, Matching, Match & Sequence, and Fill the Blank.
