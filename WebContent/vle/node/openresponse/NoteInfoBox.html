Reflecton Note
  • A Reflection Note appears as a pop-up window. You can resize this window by dragging the lower-right corner.
  • Review the question, then compose a reply in the response box.
  • Click Save to save your work. Click Edit to revise your work. Click Save & Close to save and close the Reflection note in a single click.
  • You can revise your work an unlimited number of times, to create the best response possible!
