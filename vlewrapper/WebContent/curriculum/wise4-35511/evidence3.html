Forms of Energy

Energy appears in many forms. The following are the forms of energy involved in cars:

  • Chemical energy is energy that is given off by a chemical reaction. When gasoline is burned inside the engine of cars, the burning releases chemical energy to power the car.
  • Mechanical energy moves objects from place to place. A car uses mechanical energy when it moves from your home to the school.
  • Heat energy is heat. The burning of gasoline also releases chemical energy as heat to warm up the engine and the car.
  • Light energy is light. Electrical energy is electricity. Chemical reactions happening in the battery of a car gives off energy as electricity to lighten the headlights, tail lights and brake lights.
