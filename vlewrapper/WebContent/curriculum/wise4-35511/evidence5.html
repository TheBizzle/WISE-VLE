Energy Forever?

Energy sources fall into two groups:

  • Non-renewable energy sources:
    sources that can run out someday, such as the fossil fuels. Fossil fuels need millions of years to form. If we keep using more and more fossil fuels, one day they will run out.
 
  • Renewable energy sources:
    sources of energy that will never run out. For example, the sun and wind are renewable energy sources.
