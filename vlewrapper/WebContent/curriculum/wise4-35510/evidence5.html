Hydrogen Model

Why did the hydrogen balloon explode so violently? What happened to the molecules of hydrogen and oxygen inside the balloon?

You will use a powerful molecular simulation to see what happens during the burning of hydrogen at an atomic scale in a very, very slow pace. In this activity, we will 1) explore the model, and 2) understand how the reaction happens.

While you play with the simulation, keep in mind the chemical reaction equation for the burning of hydrogen.
