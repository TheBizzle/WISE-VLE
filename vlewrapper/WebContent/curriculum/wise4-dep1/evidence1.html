This online activity was designed by researchers to help students learn about some particularly challenging kinds of concepts that appear in many different areas of science and engineering. By participating in this activity, you will develop a better understanding of these concepts, which should help you in future engineering courses.

We appreciate your careful responses to all the problems and reflection prompts in this online project, which are very important to our continuing research. To get started, first complete the demographic questionnaire that follows and then try your hand at some interesting problems in “Test your understanding”.
